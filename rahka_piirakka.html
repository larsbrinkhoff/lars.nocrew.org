Rahka piirakka

Finnish cheese pie

Ingredients:

Pie dough

  • 100 gram margerine
  • 0.75 dl sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2.5 dl flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Filling

  • 1 - 2 dl sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1.5 dl Kesella (or Creme Fraiche or something like that)
  • 1 dl cream or milk

Preparation:

Preheat the oven to 175 C (350 F). Mix the ingredients for the pie dough. Form it to a ball, and put it in the fridge for about 30 minutes. Spread it in a pie pan. Bake it a little while making the filling.

Mix the ingredients for the filling. Pour it into the pie dough shell. Bake it for abuot 25 - 30 minutes.
