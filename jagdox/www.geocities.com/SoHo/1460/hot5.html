Nat's 5 HOT test Albums

Van Halen, 1984

The worlds best guitarist playing the best guitar moments in rock's history.One highlight after the other until the last two songs. Which would have fit better on one of the earlier four records (which are all great also).
Ministry, Psalm 69

This record set the standards for heavy riffing in the nineties. A standard that hasn't been surpassed although many have tried. But perfection can at best be matched. Just one fix,just one fix
Prodigy, Music for the Jilted Generation

Its very rare nowadays to buy a record with more than two outstanding songs on it. This record is almost completely full of them. Energetic, innovative, highly danceable. Music can't do any better than this.
Joe Satriani, Surfing with the Alien

Incredible soloing on top of very good songs indeed. I read in a guitar magazine article once, that Satriani had to stop playing this aggressive style, because he clenched his teeth so tightly during play, that he got a serious health disorder. On the solos in "Surfing" and "Ice 9" , you know when its teeth-clenching time. Best solos in record history.
Front 242, Front by Front

The beat masters from belgium. This is the only great record Front 242 ever made, a landmark in electronic music. Polyrhytmic electronic mayhem with skinhead imagery and brutal vocal delivery. It's that good, that there isn't even a distant second in EBM territory in sight.
